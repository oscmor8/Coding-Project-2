About

Minorities and Financial Literacy

In our society, there is a Racial Gap in Financial Literacy; a term used to describe the social injustice of disparity in financial literacy between racial groups; thus creating disparity in stability and wealth.


Here at Jumpstart, our mission is to provide the youth of minority communities access to Financial Literacy; in efforts to close the Racial Gap!
