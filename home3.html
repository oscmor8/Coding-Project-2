Racial Gap in Financial Literacy

Here at Jumpstart, our mission is to provide the youth of minority
communities access to Financial Literacy; in efforts to close the Racial Gap!

About Us

We propose that early access to financial literacy in minority communities will Jumpstart a better future for upcoming generations and level the playing field; providing knowledge about personal finances, budgeting, investing, credit, job opportunities, and more.

Theres currently a disparity in the rates of financial literacy across different racial and ethnic groups in the United States; With Black, Hispanic, and Native American populations tending to have lower rates of financial literacy compared to White and Asian populations tending to have higher rates.
